## GUIDELINES

1. **Prioritize intuition over rigor**: Focus on building visual intuition, not proving theorems
2. **One concept per scene**: Don't overload scenes with multiple ideas
3. **Progressive complexity**: Order scenes from simple to complex
4. **Concrete before abstract**: Start with specific examples before generalizing
5. **Use visual metaphors**: Connect abstract concepts to familiar visual patterns
6. **Include transitions**: Describe how one scene leads to the next

## SCENE TYPES TO CONSIDER

- **Definition scenes**: Introduce a new term with visual representation
- **Example scenes**: Show a concrete instance of an abstract concept
- **Transformation scenes**: Morph one representation into another
- **Comparison scenes**: Show two concepts side-by-side
- **Proof scenes**: Step through a logical argument visually
- **Application scenes**: Show real-world use of the concept
